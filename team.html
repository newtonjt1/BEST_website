Trinitas Tech Robotics consists of approximately eight students from Trinitas Classical Academy in Alabama. The group has competed in the Northwest Alabama BEST (Boosting Engineering, Science, and Technology) Robotics Competition since 2017. The competition is held annually in the fall. At the competition, the team of middle and high school students will compete in several different areas: a head to head robotics competition, engineering notebook, marketing presentation, team exhibit and interview, and spirit and sportsmanship.
